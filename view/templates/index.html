Project Overview

The COVID-19 virus pandemic continues to blaze a trial of upheaval and a path of destruction. In the US alone, tens of thousands of lives have been loss and millions of other persons are at risk for infection. As cities across the country practice shelter-in-place, businesses that fall outside the definition of non-essential have been forced to close their doors and implement widespread remote working and telecommuting standards.

Objective

In recognition of the work from home mandates and forced closing of businesses (such as restaurants, salons, clothing stores), our team decided to conduct an analysis to better determine the impact COVID-19 is having on jobs market â€“ more specifically the viability of the data science job market.

Scope

As part of our analysis, our team chose to focus on the following 5 cities:

  1. New York
  2. Chicago
  3. Boston
  4. San Francisco
  5. Seattle
Not only are these cities the largest employers of Data Scientist, they are also major cities among the states with the most reported cases of the COVID-19 virus (source: NY Times).
