Covid-19 Impact on Data Science Jobs

Job Postings Impact USA

The first COVID-19 cases in the US began 59 days ago on February 21st. From the graphs provided, it is clear, that as new cases of COVID-19 goes up the job postings are, overall, going down.

Jobs/Covid Impact USA

The Covid-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on job postings including those for data science roles. The negative correlation continues to be strengthened over time due to shelter in place rules.

Jobs/Covid Impact NY

New York's first cases of COVID-19 started 42 days ago on March 9th. It is clear that as the situation in NY gets worse the job oppurtunities decline.

Jobs/Covid Impact CA

California's first cases of Covid started 46 days ago (March 5th) and was among the first states to implement shelter in place rules. This seemingly contributed to minimal job postings in California.
